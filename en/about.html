I’m a multifaceted designer, with a strong hybrid profile. I strongly believe that an accessible and clean design can make a product, unique.

With 5+ years of experience in the field, I understand how to prioritize the client needs and put the user at the center of the design. Throughout my years of experience as a designer, I have worked on many projects and been part of many teams, which helped me learn about different work dynamics and nurture myself from many talented people and valuable design resources. I work daily with Adobe Package tools and front-end languages to help create campaigns that covers all fronts.

Being part of a team is important to me. As a designer I believe that together we are stronger, each person in a team has something valuable to contribute, and as designers it is essential to empathize with our colleagues, co-workers, leaders as well as with our clients.
