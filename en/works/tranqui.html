Tranqui

Tranqui is an app that offers its users a centralized fundraising space where all participants can contribute financially. The app allows you to create different groups for different events that require fundraising. Users can record detailed shopping lists and bills, with full visibility for all group participants.

I conducted research to validate the problem and the subsequent competitor analysis. I conducted interviews with potential app users and developed a meaningful and concise problem statement (PSO). I then identified the app's essential features and designed the information architecture, task flow, and user flow. I designed wireframes in all fidelities, carried out prototyping, and conducted usability testing. I also performed heuristic evaluation and UX metrics.

Date: September 2024

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop & Figma

Role: UX Research & UI Design
