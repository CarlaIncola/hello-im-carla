Best Buy

As part of the creative production team at VML, I collaborated on the design and animation of digital campaigns for Best Buy. My role focused on producing high-quality digital display materials for seasonal promotions and brand initiatives, maintaining full consistency with Best Buy's visual identity and marketing goals.

I was responsible for designing and producing weekly Top Deals campaigns, as well as animated advertising banners and static graphics for ad placements. Top deals consisted of a set of six interactive banners per week for the holidays, built in Adobe Animate and refined through HTML, ensuring optimal functionality and brand fidelity.

Date: August 2022 - March 2023
