Fenty Beauty

A self-initiated redesign exploring how Fenty Beauty’s digital presence could evolve through a more refined UX and elevated visual design. The goal was to capture the brand’s inclusive, confident spirit while creating a smoother, more immersive shopping experience.

This is an unofficial, self-initiated design study inspired by publicly available visuals and products from Fenty Beauty.
